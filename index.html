The big difference between being human and human being

We are a foundation against animal abuse, more specifically of dogs and the greyhound breed. Since governments and public institutions do the minimum to help this group, we decided to bet on the world of blockchain where people are compromised and there is no possible way to scam the market. 20% of the profits will go to donations, which we will constantly make and publish the evidence of the donation, both customer opinions and partner companies from where we will get the material to donate.

STAKE. Digging Events

The big chance to "dig" rewards

The better your rarity, the better your chances of getting rewards. There are multipliers that you can use to earn rewards.

The user will deposit their nfts in a Smart Contract to get the Digpower, and will start to get rewards for 21 epochs (21 days) in which their nfts will be blocked. During these 21 epochs your "pending treasure" will appear that at the end of the period you can claim or start a new "digging process".

This is a practical example: you have bought 9 nfts, and you have 3 commons, 3 rare, 2 epic and 1 legend. Your Digpower will be = (3x100) + (3x150) + (2x275) + (1x500) = 1800.

You have the chance to dig with minimum 3 NFTs and maximum 9 NFTs. You can find the different rarities and get the best diggpower in a 10k NFTs collection.
